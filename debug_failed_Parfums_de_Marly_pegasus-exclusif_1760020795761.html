Elegant & cheap
This Eau de Parfum is a pleasant surprise in this price range, something that the brand is already leaving as a good record.
Excellent in the starting notes, which gradually becomes a very elegant and pleasant perfume for daily use.
Unfortunately, its longevity is not the best, but this less good attribute is compensated by the fantastic quality/price ratio (13€/100 ml).
It's use and abuse.

Pleasant and very balanced perfume, at an unbeatable price for the quality.
