Body lotion?
I like to give drugstore fragrances a chance and always smell my way through the range when I'm shopping at the red horse man. The Carlo Colucci fragrances on the bottom shelf have always caught my attention, but unfortunately I just can't make friends with them. I've sprayed the Signature on a test strip so many times now and explored the entire pyramid again and again, trying to explore it as a whole and all its elements, but I just can't find the said signature in it. I think too much of a certain body lotion every time I hold it under my nose. I wish I'd never smelled it, maybe I'd feel differently now. I don't know if it's those sweaters that have somehow done it to me or the design of the back of the bottle. Unfortunately, it will probably never be the fragrance, no matter how hard I try...
