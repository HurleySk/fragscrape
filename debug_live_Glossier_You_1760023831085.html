Sailor Neptune X Sailor Uranus
Glossier’s You is a scent I really had no intention of ever buying, but then my curiosity got the best of me. A minor point: I hate this bottle, it’s dreadful. It looks like a small pink blandly Cronenbergian lump of quivering flesh. I can, however, get over that, because as it turns out and much to my surprise...I actually really love what’s inside the horrid skin sack of a bottle. It’s possible that I had very low expectations because I don’t like any of Glossier’s other products and also because I am maybe a snob. But I really don’t mind being wrong! Okay, I am a Taurus and I hate being wrong! But I make an exception for perfume. You is a wonderful melding of this chilly, ghostly delicate iris musk and a warm, woody, sturdy peachy amber quietly enveloped in a crystalline psychic glow of pink pepper and you kind of wonder how these notes got together but then you think of Sailor Neptune and Sailor Uranus and it all just makes perfect sense. Yes, this is a queer classic anime power couple of a scent and I absolutely adore it.

Great everyday scent
You've probably heard of the "your lips but better" kind of lipstick - this perfume is the equivalent of that in regards to scent. It opens up with peppery - fresh - fruity notes, and then develops into a softer, more powdery "clean" scent that smells almost like your skin - only better.

"You" definitely sticks hovers close to your skin, and is a comforting companion over the day. It doesn't announce its presence, but is definitely noticeable for those close to you - especially in hugs, where it tends to get compliments of the "you smell so good today" variety.

For me, this is a great everyday scent: relaxed, clean, calm, but still fresh. However, if you haven't owned a scent of the category "skin scent" yet, I'd definitely advise sampling this before, as I've heard some people say they cannot notice this scent on their skin at all.

You is Me
Scent: This perfume definitely is one of those YMMV scents. I mean, it’s kind of their whole gimmick, right? A fragrance that’s personalized to you? But here’s the thing: I really like it. I didn’t expect to. When I first sprayed it it didn’t smell like much. But I found I kept coming back to the sample and the bit I accidentally sprayed on my hand. It’s just a perfect scent for me. It is heavy on Ambroxan, and if that’s something you like, you’ll probably like this. I am utterly enchanted by ambroxan and generally love iris so this is kind of perfect for me. It has a very cozy, creamy feel on my skin which makes it highly comforting…to me. On someone else, I could easily see this coming off as too powdery and uninteresting. Of all the ambroxan heavy fragrances, this is one of my favorites after Molecule 2. 9/10

Longevity: Don’t expect this to hang around long. It has a lifetime of maybe 4 hours if I’m lucky, and that’s with several spritzes. You will go through a lot of fragrance. 5/10

Sillage: This is also called You because this fragrance is for you. It is very much a skin scent so unless you do a lot of snuggling you’ll be the main one appreciating it. 5/10

Bottle: I like the indentation on the bottle. But why the plastic cap? Boo. 4/10

Value: Worth giving it a whirl. I keep a small bottle of it mostly for when I go to bed. It’s might not be worth it if you use it frequently. 6/10
