Sweet and thick nectar-like mango
This is not a fresh mango scent for summer. It's a very sweet, think thick nectar-like mango juice fit for colder weather due to its very apparent sweetness. Probably what I wanted Fame Eau de Parfum to smell like.

The beginning is very mango forward: I see myself biting down a ripe mango with its juices dripping down my chin. Sweet, juicy, fruity and a bit overwhelming if I'm being honest. Blackberry adds some tartness while black pepper offers a lovely depth and dimension to the scent. As it moves forward, mango is ever so present, but I start getting florals. Creamy jasmine and a slightly powdery and fresh iris. I also start getting some very strong sweetness, probably from the sugar and the vanilla. Vanilla gives sugary warmth to the fragrance while patchouli is very subtle and adds a little bit of woodiness at the edge of the scent.

Overall it's a nice fruity fragrance featuring mango, but I found it a tad too sugary sweet to my nose. It's more of an in-your-face-fragrance than what I'm used to from Vilhelm Parfumerie. i'm also trying to figure out what fruits I wish to wear on my skin and I'm starting to realize mango might not be one of them. I do suggest you try it out for yourself though!

challenging if you're sensitive to patchouli
Although I do ultimately like this fragrance, I nearly choked on the initial spray and coughed for quite a while. That sounds terribly melodramatic, but I've never had an experience like that before! I had a really hard time with the patchouli and black pepper at the forefront. The mango note is nice and juicy, although a little synthetic. The perfume itself took a little longer than I would have liked for it to settle into something sweeter and less suffocating.

I know this might not sound like a positive review, but it is! It's a green, spicy, complex mango that I really do like, but I cannot envision myself wearing this often because the opening is so difficult for me to get past. It is worth noting that I'm extremely sensitive to patchouli! I purchased a decanter fully aware of this fact, but couldn't get over the rest of the notes, so I looked past it! I think if you're looking for a mango scent with a little extra something, this might be worth a try!
