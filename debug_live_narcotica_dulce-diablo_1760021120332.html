A winter favourite!
This smells exactly of boozy, chocolate covered apricots. Such a unique and very well blended scent. The dry down is also divine! Someone said it isn’t really a gourmand, but you don’t get any more gourmand than this! It literally smells as though you’ve taken a bowl, chopped up some fresh apricots, then drizzled them in cognac, honey and chocolate. It is so mouth watering and delicious. I’m not a fan of apricots, yet I really adore this scent! So much so I’ve invested in a full bottle, because I just couldn’t stop thinking of this perfum. There just isn’t anything like this, or anything that can compare to this on the market. It’s a masterpiece in my opinion and the best scent from this house.

Best Apricot Scent!
My gosh, this is good!! I did not know that I could love apricot so much in a perfume until I tried Dulce Diablo! I do not get the tootsie roll vibe like some people claim to (thank goodness because I am not a fan of tootsie rolls and never have been), and it doesn't come off as too boozy to me either, which is something else that can turn me off. The blend is perfection because all I get is sweet, slightly boozy, honeyed apricots! And it's delicious!! I wouldn't say it's $280 worth of delicious but it smells so yummy! Once I spray Dulce Diablo on it's there until I shower, so I am super happy with the longevity! And that bottle...wow, just stunning! So heavy and luxe! Highly recommend if you love sweet, slightly boozy apricot perfumes and don't mind dropping a pretty penny on one.

Love Dulce Diablo
Thank you, Master Perfumer Claude Dir. This one is a masterpiece. Chocolate and apricot and rich, soft sandalwood and honey drench in Irish and expensive cognac and rum, wow. I have been using niche perfumes for many years, and I rarely get compliments. This one turned heads for me. Few people said I smelled delicious, and few asked what perfume I was wearing. I m in love with this one and will probably get a second backup bootle.
