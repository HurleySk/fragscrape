Yoga retreat in the Maldives
When I tested this last October, I wasn’t sure of this. I usually prefer sweet feminine fragrances and steer away from unisex scents. While I liked this, there was something harsh about it I didn’t enjoy and just couldn’t see myself wearing it. I also felt it was a little masculine for me, however I went on the initial spray and scrubbed it off.

Fast forward to now… it’s summer and it’s hot. This scent is absolutely perfect for high heat. On skin, the initial opening I felt was too much for me melts away into this beautiful, uplifting, light, refreshing scent. I smell the mint, mango, pineapple and Labdanum. I absolutely love the mint and mango mix. This makes this a perfect choice for hot weather. I don’t usually like Labdanum either, but in this scent it gives the fragrance an oceanic type of vibe without it being too heavily aquatic. To me this conjures up a picture of sitting by the sea in the Maldives, meditating at some posh, fancy yoga retreat. There’s incense burning, the waves are rolling, there’s a table nearby with a spread of freshly sliced, juicy sweet mango’s and pineapples, and jugs of icy water laced with fresh mint and cucumber. I don’t know what it is about this scent, but I now find it incredibly addictive, calming and relaxing! It’s absolutely stunning and easily a new summer fav!

A thousand and one nights...
First of all, I apologize for my German
Shanthi, or Sasva in general, are unfortunately, or fortunately, fragrances that don't get the hype they should.
From das House I have tried Shanthi, Fruit du Paradis and Garten of youth.
All three are, in my opinion, fragrances that are really really good.

I decided to buy Shanthi after receiving a sample from a friend.
Shanthi is a fragrance that you can actually always use, a fruity, floral gourmand fragrance.
Immediately after spraying, you can perceive the spicy notes of cardamom and pepper. Mint is also immediately present, providing a very pleasant freshness.
After these first seconds, the fruits come out really well balanced with salty notes. It's not a sweet fragrance that you can't use during the hot days, everything fits so well together.
The composition stays on my skin for hours, you notice it but it doesn't bother you!

The fragrance makes you feel at peace with yourself.

Maffei has come up with another great fragrance.

Real mango
I never had the brand on my radar until I received a bottle from a nice perfumer a few days ago The scent immediately thrilled me, this fresh mango that simply smells "real" with a hint of mint, which is also noticeable throughout the course is really a dream.
Every now and then the pineapple scents around the corner together with the patchouli.
Amberwood rounds off the whole thing, giving you a fruity, fresh fragrance that still comes across as classy! Anyone looking for fruity, non-artificial summer fragrances should really give Shanthi a try.
The fragrance is definitely suitable for men and women, I think it deserves more attention!
