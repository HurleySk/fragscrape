Old fashionned fragrance tiziana terenzi
discovered following a blind purchase, it was a gift to my wife for the holidays.

given the success of this brand with already telea and draco very appreciated by madam I thought once again to have found the perfect gift.

then the bottle is magnificent but then the smell is very special it is really very green perhaps because of the rosemary we also smell blood orange and grapefruit, but there is a smell that is a bit old lady in addition the sillage is strong and heady.

the sumptuous bottle justifies part of the price but the perfume does not. I advise you not to buy it blind.

It is clearly a feminine perfume and for a fairly mature lady, I would say at least 50 years old.

I hope this review has been useful to you.
