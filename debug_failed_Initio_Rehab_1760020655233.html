snow covered sauna
Nordic Cedar feels like the color silver. It’s a sharp, cold breath of fresh air in a snowy cedar grove. The cardamom and patchouli give it a spicy greenness in the opening, but in a way that feels cold and airy. It almost borders on minty to my nose. The musk is as sheer and white as musk can get.

It feels like stepping out of a dry cedar sauna into the brisk winter air. Minimal, clean, and polished, but not bland.
