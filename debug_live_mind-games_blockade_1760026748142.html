Great cinema without a screen
This fragrance was one of the few that managed to inspire me from the very first spray. I was actually hooked immediately :)

The vibrant and surprisingly unsweet mango is unique and long-lasting. Blockade effortlessly masters the fine art of being "inconspicuously conspicuous" in the character of Springer. Great cinema without a screen.
The top note is vibrantly fresh and yet absolutely elegant. A feel-good fragrance that doesn't take itself too seriously and yet can be taken very seriously. A fragrance of contradictions that is perfectly balanced. Sounds crazy and like a random mess? The fragrance is crazy, but it is simply PERFECTLY balanced.

As the top note barely fades on my skin, the good mood lingers. It is merely underpinned with slightly spicy fougère elegance (lavender and star anise) and then lays itself gently on a rather attractively alluring ambrostar-leather-oud-patchouli bed (read, but actually smelled :)).
Personally, I have never smelled such an uncomplicated yet highly complex fragrance.

I'm matte set. From a jumper. And with great pleasure!

(PS: The previous reviews by Loukas and Max815 describe the fragrance wonderfully. But I simply HAD to add my two cents :))

Blockade - The perfect symbiosis of green freshness and long-lasting elegance
Blockade by Mind Games captivated me from the first application and immediately cast a spell over me. This perfume combines incredible complexity with a refreshingly clean, green mango scent that is both exceptional and long-lasting.

What makes Blockade stand out for me is the present mango note, which is not only fruity, but also surprisingly green and fresh. This special combination gives the fragrance a striking liveliness that is immediately noticeable, both to yourself and to those around you.
From the very first spray, this note unfolds without being too dominant and blends harmoniously with the other nuances of the perfume. The mango remains at the heart of the fragrance and surprises above all with its extraordinary longevity. Even after several hours, it is still easily perceptible and ensures that the fragrance retains its freshness and dynamism.

The sillage is very good: the fragrance has a perceptible but not intrusive projection. It leaves behind a pleasantly fresh cloud of fragrance that subtly fills the room without being too heavy. As I said, the longevity leaves nothing to be desired. On my skin, it easily lasts the whole day without fading or losing intensity.

All in all, Blockade by Mind Games is an exceptional perfume for all those who are looking for a unique, green-fresh mango fragrance that impresses with both its longevity and its sillage. This perfume manages to be effortlessly fresh, elegant and long-lasting at the same time - a rare combination that should delight any perfume lover.

If chessboards could smell
Wow what can I say

Blockade by Mindgames is a perfume that comes across as a clever move: unexpected, sophisticated and somehow ingenious. It smells as if someone has combined the elegance of a king with the repartee of a queen - only without a board and pieces.

The first spray? An attack! Fresh citrus notes that shake you awake like a "checkmate" on the first move. But then, while you're still thinking about what has just happened, the heart note comes in: Woody and leathery accords that are so classy and confident that you almost feel like you have to wear a tuxedo, even if you're just in sweatpants.

And the base? Warm, smoky and somehow... clever. Imagine sitting by the fireplace with a glass of whiskey in your hand while challenging a world chess champion. That's exactly how it feels.

Blockade is a fragrance for strategists, fragrance lovers and anyone who enjoys life with a twinkle in their eye. It manages the balancing act between elegant and casual - and remains wonderfully unforgettable.
