A sweet incense
This is a sweet, smokey incense fragrance. Notes of pear and rhubarb add a bit of fruity sweetness to the potent aromatics of olibanum (frankincense). Rhubarb also adds in a bit of tartness, and pairs well with the woody sharp notes of vetiver and oak. A lovely rose note comes on faintly in the drydown, adding a but of floral sweetness. Elemi is an event element in this which adds more olfactory beauty to this wonderful fragrance
